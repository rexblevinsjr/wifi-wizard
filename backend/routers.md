## Netgear Nighthawk R7000
Login: http://routerlogin.net
2.4GHz channel: Advanced > Setup > Wireless Setup > 2.4GHz Channel
5GHz channel: Advanced > Setup > Wireless Setup > 5GHz Channel
Smart Connect / Band Steering: Advanced > Setup > Wireless Setup > Enable Smart Connect (toggle)
QoS: Advanced > Setup > QoS Setup

## Xfinity XB7 / XB8 Gateway
Login: http://10.0.0.1
Wi-Fi settings: Connection > Wi-Fi
2.4GHz channel: Connection > Wi-Fi > Edit > 2.4GHz Channel
5GHz channel: Connection > Wi-Fi > Edit > 5GHz Channel
Band Steering: Connection > Wi-Fi > Advanced Settings > Auto (toggle)

## TP-Link Archer A7 / A8
Login: http://tplinkwifi.net
2.4GHz channel: Wireless > Wireless Settings > 2.4GHz Channel
5GHz channel: Wireless > Wireless Settings > 5GHz Channel
QoS: Advanced > QoS > Enable QoS
